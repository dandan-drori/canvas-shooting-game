Rules


The game is comprised of the following pieces:

Player, Enemies and Bonuses.

The Player:

The Player is a white circle and located at the center of the screen.

The Player dies when an Enemy hits him.

The Player may shoot Projectiles by clicking / pressing on the screen, in order to hit Enemies, which in turn will destroy them.

The Enemies:

The Enemies are colorful circles that randomly spawn at the edges of the screen.

When an Enemy hits the Player, the game is over.

The Enemies vary in size:

When a Projectile hits a small* Enemy, they both are destroyed.

When a Projectile hits a big* Enemy, the Enemy shrink and the Projectile is destroyed.

The Bonuses:

There are 6 types of Bonuses: Slow, Sharp Shooter, Explosive Rounds, Extra Life, Nuke and Skull.

Each Bonus is a hollow square, with a different color.

When a Projectile hits a Bonus, a special effect takes place, as described bellow.

Slow:

Color:

Effect: Reduce all Enemies speed by half.

Sharp Shooter:

Color:

Effect: Make the Player shoot Projectiles 20% faster.

Explosive Rounds:

Color:

Effect: When a Projectile hits an Enemy, it destroys it regardless of its size.

Extra Life:

Color:

Effect: Gain an extra life.

Nuke:

Color:

Effect: Destroy all Enemies on the screen.

Skull:

Color:

Effect: Lose game instantly, regardless of the Player's amount of life.
